like a stone, i am still.

not physically still, as my foot taps rather uninterrupted.
my fingers too, tap, tap, tap, they'll never rest.
i'm anxious to be honest, like something is wrong.
*something is wrong*
*something is wrong*
*something is wrong*

what?
nothing.

really though, there's nothing wrong inherently.
i'm rather okay, and things are going well.
well, okay, they're not, but . . .

the feeling of not being able to do anything
solely because anything is not that something
there's that something i need to do, but
i won't

why?
no reason.

at least, not a good one!
i mean, i want to do that thing. i really do.
how many times do i have to shout it out?
*i really want to!*
*i really want to!*
*i really want to!*

so why don't i?
if i knew, i would've done it already.
yet here i am, anxiously tapping away
thinking to myself how much i want to do
all the things i need to do
and watching myself do

**nothing**
